Data type:
1. Int: number without decimals - 2 or 4 bytes
2. Double: floating point number, with decimals - 8 bytes
3. Char: single characters. Surrounded by single quotes - 1 byte
4. String: text. Surrounded by double quotes
5. Bool: true or false - 1 byte
6. Float: Stores fractional numbers, containing one or more decimals. - 4 bytes
